Welcome To The Department Of Applied Mathematics

Welcome to the Department of Applied Mathematics at the University Of FID. The Department of Mathematics is one of the founding departments of the University Of FID which opened its door and started functioning on July 1, 1921. From the establishment of the University Of FID, as well as the Department of Mathematics students have been awarded M. Sc degree in Applied Mathematics and Pure Mathematics. Applied Mathematics has started its journey as an individual Department from the current academic year.

The Department of Applied Mathematics is an academic unit of the Faculty of Science of the University Of FID. Its mission is to provide the appropriate educational and research infrastructure for the study of mathematical and computational tools broadly applicable in science and engineering, and on their use in solving challenging problems in these and related fields. A sound knowledge of mathematical tools makes a technocrat to excel in his profession.

Department of Applied Mathematics at the University Of FID provides a dynamic and engaging graduate training environment that is especially strong in developing advanced computational methods. The Department currently consists of 11 faculty members. Among them there are 2 professors, 1 associate professor, 3 assistant professors and 2 lecturers. All of them are actively involved in research of different areas of Applied Mathematics.
